The Art of the Loom

Every Kabul Heritage carpet is a masterpiece that takes months to complete. Utilizing centuries-old techniques, our artisans spin local wool and dye it using natural elements found in the Afghan landscape—from pomegranate peels to madder root.

The result is not just a floor covering, but a generational heirloom. The lanolin-rich wool ensures your carpet is naturally stain-resistant, incredibly soft underfoot, and develops a beautiful, glowing patina over the decades.

  • 100% Hand-spun Afghan Wool
  • Natural Botanical Dyes
  • Direct from Artisans to You
